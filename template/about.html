ABOUT US


Deevee equipments established in 2015, is a leading technology driven company based at Coimbatore in Tamil Nadu, India. We are the Sole Proprietorship based company that is involved in the manufacturing, wholesaling and trading of Water Level Controller, Water Pump Controller and Mobile Starter and many more. The main objective of our company is to provide a complete solution for water management automation system for domestic, industrial and agricultural sector. We are one of the reputed manufacturers, service provider, suppliers and retailers of a vast array of Embedded Development products that is used for agricultural and industrial purpose.




our firm is engaged as the manufacturer of Dryrun preventer, Cyclic Timer, GSM Mobile Starter, Auto Start preventer, Street light timers , Drip valve controllers ,Water Level controller and various type of Agricultural panels and Starters many more and service providers of Electronic Project Services. We are one of the leading automatic water pump controller manufacturing company. We are also pioneers in bringing mobile based automatic water pump controller and starters which has made a tremendous impact in the agricultural sector. We also introduced India’s most advanced GSM based starter controller with voice response system in various regional languages.



We also provide customized products such as automatic water pump controller for residential houses, apartment’s, multiplex, hospitals, hotels, schools, colleges and many other places and customized timers for hoarding lights and street lights. The company has also provided solution to automation in drip irrigation system mobile based for revolution in agriculture field. The company has its technology center with dynamic young aspirants, thriving and willing to provide solutions to the challenging problems encountered. Additionally, we provide PCB development Services. The products offered by us exhibits the superior quality as per the set industrial specifications.



Our products are the first choice of our clients due to their performance product features and quality Standards like precise robust Construction, designs, long service life, optimum Performance, smooth operations and anti-corrosive finish and high tensile strength. The advanced techniques we use while production is the only reason we have been able to cater to the ever rising market demands which also helps us to attain maximum levels of goodwill in the market..
